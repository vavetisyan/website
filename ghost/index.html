Ghost Wallpaper

Step into the mysterious world of Ghost Wallpaper, designed for fans of metal, rock, and dark aesthetics. Each wallpaper carries an eerie yet artistic vibe — perfect for those who love something bold and different. Tired of plain, boring backgrounds? Bring your screen to life with haunting beauty and a touch of mystery.

App Features

  • High-resolution Ghost wallpapers for your phone.
  • Easy one-tap setup for home screen and lock screen.
  • Regular updates with fresh, stunning images.
  • Lightweight and fast-loading app.

How to Use

  1. Open the app and browse the gallery.
  2. Select the wallpaper you love.
  3. Tap "Set as Home Screen" to set it on your home screen.
  4. Tap "Set as Lock Screen" to set it on your lock screen.
