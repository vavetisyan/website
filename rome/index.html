About Rome

Rome is a historic city known as the capital of Italy and one of the most important cultural centers in Europe. The city is famous for landmarks such as the Colosseum, the Roman Forum, and the Vatican. Ancient architecture and centuries of history make Rome a fascinating place visited by millions of travelers every year.

Rome wallpapers often feature beautiful historical buildings, warm Mediterranean colors, and dramatic city views. These backgrounds bring a classic European atmosphere to modern Android devices.
