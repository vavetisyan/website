About Washington DC

Washington DC is the capital city of the United States and an important center for politics and history. The city is known for famous landmarks such as the White House, the Capitol Building, and the Lincoln Memorial.

Washington DC wallpapers often feature grand monuments, wide avenues, and beautiful nighttime lighting. These backgrounds give smartphones a clean and powerful city atmosphere.
